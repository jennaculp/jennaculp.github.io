Seafood - My Favorite Cuisine

Why I Love Seafood

Seafood is one of my favorite types of food because it's fresh, flavorful, and versatile! Whether it's grilled salmon, buttery lobster, or spicy seafood boils, there's always something delicious to enjoy. Plus, it's a great source of protein and omega-3 fatty acids!
